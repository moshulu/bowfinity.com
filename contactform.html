CUSTOM ORDERING

When ordering a custom bow from BOWfinity Cheer Bows you will need to fill out a Custom Bow Request Form. After the order form is submitted we will contact you to go over your custom request needs. At that point we will start working on a mock up of your bow requests. After the mock ups are completed, they will be sent to you for approval. Production will begin once the approval is signed off on and payment is received.

We would also like the Bow template order form to be available here for them to draw and design the custom bow and email the sheet back to us.

Please describe your custom bow. -Custom Bow Templates can be emailed to BOWfinityCheerBows@gmail.com. Please insert your name and or gym name in the subject line.

** If you need your item by a certain date within normal shipping times, please include the date so we can accommodate you. This date needs to be before your event, competition or game. Arrangements cannot be made without a specific date.
