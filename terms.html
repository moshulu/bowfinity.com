Payment Policy

All custom orders have 48 hours from the time the order was approved to make a full payment on their invoice. Payments that are not made within 48 hours are subject to cancellation.

Processing Time

Processing time is the amount of time it takes to make your cheer bows, and is completely separate from shipping time. Standard processing time is 14 business days for individual orders up to 10 bows. For large or team orders of 10 or more processing times may vary and will be determined on how detailed your order will be. If your order is completed before the standard processing time, you will be notified via email that your order will be shipped early.

Rush Orders or Emergencies

We understand that sometimes things happen and you need a replacement bow ASAP! Each situation is different so it's best to contact us immediately regarding your emergency via email! Rush orders can be accommodated and additional charges will be determined on a case by case basis. Processing times for rush orders will be 7 business days.

Refund Policy

ALL SALES ARE FINAL!!

If by chance your order does not match your approved design as indicated on your invoice or web order, you must contact BOWfinity Cheer Bows within 7 days from the date of confirmed delivery by USPS. Upon approval by BCB and the receipt of the returned item, a new item will be produced based on the new requested information. The customer is responsible for all shipping costs associated with the re-made item.

You must send your refund request to BOWfinityCheerBows@gmail.com. Please include “REFUND REQUEST” in the subject line. You must include in writing the issues with your order. Refund requests must be submitted in writing and will only be approved after all attempts to correct the issue have been made.

Changes to your Order & Cancellation Policy

Changes to your order- CHANGES MUST BE MADE WITHIN 24 HOURS OF RECEIPT OF APPROVED DESIGN. Any changes to an order must be made in writing via email and sent to BOWfinityCheerBows@gmail.com. Please put “ORDER CHANGE” in the subject line. Be sure to include your name/organization/gym name and your invoice number. Change requests after the 24 hour period are subject to BCB’s approval.
